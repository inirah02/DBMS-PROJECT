Trek Planning Feature

Description of Feature

The trek organizer can keep track of the number of trekkers, transport options planned, homestays, and any equipment that needs to be rented out.

Entities Involved

  • Trek locations
  • Equipment
  • Homestays
  • Vehicles and drivers
  • Trek leads to lead the trek

Necessary Input Required

  • Trek selection
  • Date
  • Number of trekkers
